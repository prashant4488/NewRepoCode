Spiderman:- Miles Morals

"Everyone keeps telling me how my story is supposed to go.
Nah... I'ma do my own thing.

"Miles, being Spider-Man is a sacrifice."

Miles Morales was created by Brian Michael Bendis and Sara Pichelli in ULTIMATE FALLOUT (2011) #4, set in the Ultimate Marvel Universe.
In this world, Aaron steals the genetically engineered spider, developed from Peter Parker's blood, and Miles is bitten by it while visiting his uncle.
